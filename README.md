# Digital Subscriber Engagement and Content Operations Transformation for Media & Entertainment Platform

This capstone project focuses on designing a Salesforce solution for StreamSphere Media, a rapidly growing digital entertainment company. The project addresses the challenges of managing subscriber relationships, content licensing, advertising partnerships, and customer support interactions across multiple disconnected systems. Students will assume the role of a Salesforce Business Analyst to analyze current-state processes, define business requirements, and propose a Salesforce-based solution to improve subscriber lifecycle management, customer engagement tracking, service case management, and executive reporting.

The project aims to consolidate subscriber data, streamline marketing efforts, enhance customer service efficiency, and provide comprehensive reporting capabilities. Students will create user personas, design future-state processes, develop a Salesforce data model, define automation strategies, and design reporting dashboards. The capstone culminates in a comprehensive Salesforce Business Analysis engagement tailored to the Entertainment and Media industry, including security access models, Agile delivery planning, UAT coordination, and a governance framework.
